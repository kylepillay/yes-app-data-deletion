Yes4Youth Data Deletion Instructions

Follow these steps to delete your data from our system. Please read all instructions carefully before proceeding.

Step 1: Request Data Deletion from Yes4Youth Support

You will need to send an email to youth@yes4youth.co.za.

The subject of the email should be "Data Deletion Request". Please include your Yes4Youth username in the email and written confirmation that you would like your data deleted.

Note: Once you confirm the deletion, your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Step 2: Receive Confirmation

After confirming your request, you will receive an email confirmation that your data deletion request has been processed. Please allow up to 24 hours for the process to complete.

If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please contact our support team at youth@yes4youth.co.za.
